LIFESTYLE

Lifestyle mapping is a plan that aligns life decisions with one's personal values, interests, and aspirations. Properly planning a work to life balance will allow you to construe a comfortable financial situation while living the lifestyle you prefer. Lifestyle matters involve balancing work and leisure to make smart decisions for the future. By combining financial goals with personal values, lifestyle investing seeks to create a more holistic and fulfilling approach to wealth management while "enjoying the journey."
